About Us

PR Foundation was born to bridge the gap between the people who want to make a difference through giving back and those who are
doing phenomenal work but need more support. Our focus has been to build trust for the social sector by strong due diligence of
all our nonprofit partners, and transparency on how donations impacted lives on the ground.
